Our Innovation

A proactive, intelligent approach to managing and preventing eczema.

Why SkinGuard?

The Problem

Eczema affects millions, especially children and teens. Families need a personalized, simple and reliable way to monitor skin health and prevent painful flare-ups that disrupt daily life.

Existing Alternatives

Ointments and hospital visits only address symptoms after they appear. Current solutions lack prevention, personalization, and consistent monitoring.

Our Solution

Real-Time Monitoring

SkinGuard uses sensor data to track hydration, inflammation and environmental triggers.

AI-Powered Insights

Our AI provides personalized advice—when to moisturize, avoid triggers, and prevent flare-ups.

Actionable History

Long-term tracking helps identify patterns and build a tailored prevention routine.

Our Unique Value

Unlike traditional treatments, SkinGuard focuses on prevention. We combine personal history, environmental data, and advanced analytics to bring eczema management into the modern era.
